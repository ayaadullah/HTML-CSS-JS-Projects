Our Heritage

Built on a Love for Classics

Founded in 2025 by a group of automotive purists, Vantage Motors has grown from a small garage in the heart of Islamabad into Pakistan's most revered destination for classic and vintage automobiles.

We believe that every classic car carries a soul — a story of its era, its engineer, and every hand that has touched it. Our mission is to preserve that story and pass it on to the next generation of enthusiasts.

Authenticated

Every car comes with full provenance documentation and independent appraisal.

Restored

Period-correct restorations by master craftsmen using original techniques.

Insured

Comprehensive collector car insurance arranged on your behalf.

Delivered

White-glove enclosed transport anywhere in Pakistan and internationally.
